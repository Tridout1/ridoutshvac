Our story

Our family owned business was started in 1996 by Dennis Ridout, lifelong resident and service technician. After well deserved retirement, the business is now owned and operated by his son, Scott Ridout. Scott, as a member of the sheet metal workers union, was a national finalist, as an apprentice, for HVAC design and fabrication. All of Ridout's Heating & Cooling technicians are required 4 years of training in the HVAC fabrication, installation, and service.

Ridout's Heating & Cooling prides themselves in treating their customers like family. Offering 24 hour emergency service in both residential and commercial industry.

Ridout's Heating & Cooling offers a wide variety, of top of line heating, air conditioning and boilers equipment. They also service all makes and models of equipment, natural gas, propane, and fuel oil. We also offer solutions for all of your indoor air quality concerns.
